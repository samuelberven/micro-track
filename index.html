- Internal Database Management System -

With this system, you can manage data concerning the company's partner developers, published games, microtransactions and their purchases, and the service providers whose platforms we publish our titles on.

  • Games Page
    Displays information concerning the titles we publish.

  • Microtransactions Page
    Displays information concerning the microtransactions available for purchase in our games.

  • Purchases Page
    Displays details for the individual purchases of our games' microtransactions.

  • Customers Page
    Displays customer information.

  • Service Platforms Page
    Displays information concerning the platforms we publish our titles on.

  • Developers Page
    Displays information about the developers that make the games.

  • Customers Have Games Page
    Displays information about the games that each customer plays.
